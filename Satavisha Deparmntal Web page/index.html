About Us

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. We offer a range of Phd and Postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of computer science.The Department of Computer Science of The University of Burdwan is successfully running M.Sc programme since 2015-16 session. The course is recognized by the University Grants Commission (U.G.C.). The Department also offers Ph.D in Computer Science in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Soft Computing and Network Security and Cryptography since 2000.

2. M.Sc in Computer Science

  • Duration:- 2 years

  • Admission Time:- July -August

  • Eligibility:- Honours graduates under (10+2+3) system with Mathematics as one of the combination subjects and having at least 50% marks in the Honours subject and also B.C.A. / B.Sc.(Computer Science) / B.Sc.(IT) / B.Sc.(E-Commerce) / B.E. / B.Tech. candidates with 60% marks are eligible to apply. Admission based on performance in the Joint Entrance Examination conducted by West Bengal Joint Entrance Board.
